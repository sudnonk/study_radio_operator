#### A-1

平行平板電極間の電解の強さ

$$
\begin{aligned}
E[V/m] = \frac{V[V]}{d[m]} \\
E = \frac{80}{0.02} = 4000
\end{aligned}
$$

電荷eに働く力の大きさ

$$
\begin{aligned}
[V] = [J/C] \\
[V/m] = [J/Cm] = [N/C]
\end{aligned}
$$

より、

$$
\begin{aligned}
f[N] = E[V/m] \times Q[C] \\
f[N] = 4000 \times -1.6*10^{-19} = -6.4 * 10^{-15}
\end{aligned}
$$

よって、答えは4。
